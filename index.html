DAYNE
HARRIS
Dayne Harris is a DJ and producer born in California and established in
Chile. Early exposure to a wide range of top-tier chilean artists helped him
develop his creative instincts. He participated in bands, performances, jazz
concerts and various other projects, learning to handle different types of
audiences.
His DJ sets and productions blend house music with a variety of
sensations, styles, and genres, mainly inspired by Hip-Hop, Soul, Funk,
Jazz, Afro, and Latin rhythms.
